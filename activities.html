    Activities

    With over 100 vendors present at the festival, there are many opportunities to be introduced to the rich artisan and culinary community in Ithaca. Our food vendors feature apple-centric and other fall classics for you to taste, and the craft vendors offer homemade goods like soaps, jewelry, sculptures, clothing, and more! This is the perfect place to learn about and support Ithaca's small businesses that encompass the heart of the town.

    Please note that many vendors only accept cash!

    Shop

    Shop from one of our seventy vendors selling a wide range of handmade items, from jewelry to clothes to plates! Support local Ithacan artists with every purchase. These items make great gifts for you or a loved one.

    Shop a variety of handmade jewelry, including earrings, rings, and necklaces!

    Eat

    If you want to grab a quick and yummy bite or dive into a delicious meal, there are plenty of food options available! Explore a range of pastries and cuisines, all prepared with love. Don't forget to check out all the apple products, whether that's an apple cider donut or caramel apple. You'll be sure to enjoy whatever you choose as you support local farmers, bakers, and chefs.

    Don't miss out on our famous apple cider donuts!
